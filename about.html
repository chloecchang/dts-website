About

Our community

EST. 2008

Drawn to Scale (DTS) is a tight-knit community of musicians and vocalists. Our repertoire ranges from pop and indie to rock, R&B, and contemporary ‚Äî brought to life through student-made arrangements and innovative choreography.

In February 2025, we became two-time ICCA semifinalists, placing 2nd at the ICCA West Quarterfinal and earning Best Choreography for the second year in a row (Kira Wefers '25, Ayanna Navarro '25, MJ Ordinario '27, Maddie McLaughlin '27). We're proud to represent UC Berkeley's growing a cappella community on the competitive stage and beyond.
