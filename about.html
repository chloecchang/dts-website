About

Our community

Drawn to Scale (DTS) is UC Berkeley's competitive co-ed a cappella group, founded in 2008. We are a tight-knit community of passionate musicians and talented singers, dedicated to making great music while letting each of our members' unique styles shine. Our repertoire ranges from pop and indie to rock, R&B, and contemporary, brought to life through student-made arrangements and innovative choreography.

In February 2025, DTS become two-time ICCA Semifinalists, placing 2nd at the ICCA West Quarterfinal, and earning Best Choreography for the second year in a row (Choreography by: Kira Wefers '25, Ayanna Navarro '25, MJ Ordinario '27, Maddie McLaughlin '27). With these accomplishments, we're proud to represent UC Berkeley's growing a cappella community on the competitive stage and beyond.
