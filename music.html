Our Music

Arranged by DTS

Latest Release

From the studio to the stage, Drawn to Scale brings new life and energy to every song through our unique interpretations. All of our arrangements are created by past and present DTS members, and we take pride in our distinct, fresh sound.

Listen to our debut single "American Boy" (opb. Estelle), now streaming on all platforms. This track was arranged by DTS alumni Bernard Kim (PhD, '19) and Timothy Fong ('21), with Ayanna Navarro ('25) as soloist.
